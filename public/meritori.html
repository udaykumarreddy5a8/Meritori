01 — What we believe

  • 01

    Talent is the only ticket.

    We don't care about your CGPA, your college tier, or your last internship. We care about what you've built, how you think, and whether you lose sleep over ideas.

  • 02

    Building beats talking.

    The best learning happens in the trenches — debugging a production bug, iterating on feedback, shipping something real. Not attending lectures on how to ship.

  • 03

    Small cohorts, real mentors.

    20 seats. Mentors who've shipped products used by real people. No 500-person bootcamp with one overwhelmed instructor — focused, deep, honest work.

  • 04

    Honesty over comfort.

    If your code is bad, we'll tell you. If your idea has a flaw, we'll find it. We're not here to make you feel good — we're here to make you genuinely better.

02 — How it works

  1. 01 ·

    Apply with honest answers

    No resume. No grades. Answer 12 questions designed to surface real builders — what you've made, why you made it, what broke, and what you learned. Takes ~15 minutes.

  2. 02 ·

    A 20-minute live build session

    Shortlisted candidates join a quick live session — not a whiteboard interview, but a real problem to think through out loud. We're evaluating curiosity, not performance.

  3. 03 ·

    Twenty are selected. The cohort begins.

    6 weeks, 6 hours/week. Weekly deep-dives, code reviews, project sprints, 1-on-1 sessions. You'll build something real by the end — not a tutorial project.

  4. 04 ·

    Ship & join the alumni network

    Graduates join a growing community of builders. Referrals, opportunities, collaborations — and the right to call yourself someone who was selected by merit alone.
